## Quick Summary

**Memory Lane** is a heartfelt Christmas gift - a 2D exploration game celebrating a baby's first year of life through interactive photo memories.

## Target Audience

- **Primary User**: Developer's wife
- **Context**: Christmas 2025 gift
- **Emotional Goal**: Nostalgia, joy, celebration of family milestones

## Core Value Proposition

Transform a collection of baby photos into an interactive, explorable journey through the first year of parenthood.

## Feature Prioritization

### Must Have (MVP)

- Baby avatar with joystick movement
- House map with collision detection
- Memory Items that trigger photo overlays
- Christmas tree end goal with montage

### Nice to Have

- Sound effects and music
- Animated baby crawling
- Multiple rooms/areas
- Progress indicator
